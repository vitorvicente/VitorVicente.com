ABOUT ME

I’m a Portuguese teenager with a passion for gaming, aviation and tech. I’m a huge fanboy and movie geek, love everything from Star Wars to Game of Thrones. I love gaming and have recently developed a passion for aviation.

I’m 18, studying Computer Science at Boston University. Graduated high school in Portugal, came to the US to study because of the culture and opportunities presented to me here.

I’ve been working alongside amateur gaming communities as a free-lancer for around 5 years now, working on every edge of the industry, from development to HR.


MY MISSION

For as cheesy as this might sound, I have it as my mission in life to help all those around me in whatever way I can, for the only thing truly worth living for is love, love of what we do, love of those who surround us, love for life.

As such I hope to one day be able to pursue a career in the tech industry that allows me to make a greater impact on the world, and all those who live in it.

Bellow is what I consider to be my favor quote, and the words I hope one day, I can live by.



Don’t ask yourself what the world needs. Ask yourself what makes you come alive, and then go do that. Because what the world needs is people who come alive.

— Howard Thurman
